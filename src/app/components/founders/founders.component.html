THE Founding Trustees

Mr. Hari Shankar Vyas is a noted figure in contemporary Hindi Journalism. With a career spanning of over 45 years, Mr. Vyas has been a fearless journalist with a daunting intellect, unwavering diligence and staunch commitment to Hindi journalistic excellence. He has always worked on how to make Journalism popular, ethical and defiant, especially Hindi journalism. He is by and by an altrustic Journalist.

Board of Trustees

The ANF Board of Trustees oversees the foundation and its work, as its ultimate corporate authority. The board is formed for a period of Three year and a few independent members of eminence and reputation are nominated each year. Members of Trust meet once every quarter.
